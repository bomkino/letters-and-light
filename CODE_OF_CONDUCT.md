# Community conduct

This project welcomes rigorous disagreement and has no use for contempt.

Treat people as peers. Critique decisions, incentives, evidence and code—not identity, disability, background or presumed competence. No harassment, stalking, sexual attention, threats, doxxing, racist or casteist behavior, misogyny, transphobia, homophobia, ableism or organised dogpiling.

Maintainers may edit, hide or remove contributions that make the project less safe or less workable, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Decisions should name the behavior and protect affected people; they should not become public punishment theatre.

Report a conduct problem privately through the security contact described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). If a maintainer is involved, say so; another maintainer should handle it where possible.
